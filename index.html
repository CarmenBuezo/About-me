My Roots

I was born in the small but proud country of Honduras, which if you don't know where it's located it's on a little strip of land that goes by the name of Central America that connects North and South America. Growing up in Honduras was great. My childhood was a mix of playing outside with mud and the neighborhood kids to enjoying every Sunday's at grandmas with the family. I was a curious child, always trying to know how things worked (breaking a few things in the process). Some things never change. Yet,it wasn't until recently that I discovered my passion for technology. I love the logic and structure that is involved in coding and manipulating data. Year Up played a big part in helping connect me with the big and ever expanding world of IT.
